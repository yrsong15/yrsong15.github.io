Nicole

Posted on June 19th, 2016

Just got back from New York, the first time we've actually met in the Big Apple - if anyone still calls New York that. It was a magical time for sure, full of adventures and that much more rewarding.

First we met up near her place and went to Times Square, more specifically the Disney shop. We initially wanted to pick up Stitch, but seeing how the Stitches were either too big or too ugly, we instead went for a Nemo, who is now safely tucked up with Nicole.

Then we headed to Central Park, where Nicole, being the angel that she is, opened up the picnic set that she prepared herself - a Nutella sandwich, plus a plethora of fruits. There we watched a little baby called Peter try to feed the pigeons. Our favorite part was when he said "Thank you Pretzel!" to the grandma who gave him the aforementioned bread, and when he just threw a whole large chunk of the pretzel onto the ground, instead of breaking it up into little pieces.
