~/raysong/workspace/me

Hi! My name's Young Re, but a lot of people just call me Ray - at least partially because far too often I've had to tell them that my name's not Young Song. As a rising junior at Duke University studying Computer Science and Economics, I aspire to empower those around me through coding and writing.

Writing has always been a part of me. Though I was born and raised in Korea for the first 19 years of my life, I've always felt in my own skin when I convey my own ideas through English writings and essays. For years I've kept a personal archive of Microsoft Word files that contain my thoughts and perspectives on a multitude of issues, but they've always been just that - an archive hidden in the depths of my own laptop.

See, the thing is, I'm not the biggest fan of posting every tidbit on social media, not only because I didn't want to spam others' News Feeds, but also because I feared that the "Likes" would make me conform to what would be expected of me to write, as opposed to what I really wanted to write. It's like writing a diary in the sense that I wouldn't mind if anyone else read it, but I also wouldn't necessarily broadcast it for everyone to see. Thus this webpage was created as a medium between my desire to write and my unwillingness to post everything on social media.

And yes, everything on this webpage has been created by me and me only, including the underlying HTML/CSS code, which has been shared on my Github account.

As for me, when I'm not making something, laboring over coursework, or FaceTiming my girlfriend(hi Nicole!!), I enjoy working out, playing pickup basketball or League of Legends, following the NBA, tenting out in K-Ville for the fabled Duke-North Carolina game, and playing with my adorable Roborovski hamster, Cheese.
